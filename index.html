About Me

I am an Arizona native of 20 plus years and enjoy living in the desert with my wife and 3 beautiful children. I currently work as a Salesman/IT Help Desk at a building supply company and spend my days checking website sales and taking phone orders.
I became interested in coding and website creation shortly after college when I discovered that I could easily create an online presence for my business from popular website creation sites such as WIX or Squarespace. For many of the website creation sites I couldnt achieve the exact look or structure that I was looking for. This led me to join a coding bootcamp from the University of Arizona and began a career transition into web development.
Best decision ever!!!
